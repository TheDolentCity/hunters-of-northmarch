---
title: Scars
description: A list of scars and their effects for D&D 4e.
---

Roll a d6 on this table to potentially gain a scar when you drop to 0 HP. Add the number of scars you currently have to the roll. Scars may increase your effectiveness at fighting, but they will eventually be the death of you.

| d6  | Result                                                                                                                                          |
| --- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1-3 | No scar.                                                                                                                                        |
| 4-6 | **Back** — A deep gouge from behind. Increase max HP by +2.                                                                                     |
| 7   | **Chest** — An ugly scar across your torso. Roll 4d6 and drop the lowest. You may replace your Constitution stat with this value.               |
| 8   | **Face** — Prominent facial scar. Roll 4d6 and drop the lowest. You may replace your Charisma stat with this value.                             |
| 9   | **Arm** — Wicked scar across your arm. Roll 4d6 and drop the lowest. You may replace your Strength or Dexterity stat with this value.           |
| 10  | **Leg** — Ropy scar snaking up your leg. Roll 4d6 and drop the lowest. You may replace your Strength or Dexterity stat with this value.         |
| 11  | **Head** — Hair refuses to grow near this scar. Roll 4d6 and drop the lowest. You may replace your Intelligence or Wisdom stat with this value. |
| 10  | **Mutilated Arm** — Your arm is useless, or removed. You have -10 to any action benefited by the use of two arms.                               |
| 11  | **Mutilated Leg** — Your leg is useless, or removed. You cannot fight or move at any reasonable speed without aid.                              |
| 12  | **Mutilated Head** — Your head is destroyed. You are killed instantly.                                                                          |
| 13+ | **Doom** — The next time you drop to 0 HP, you die.                                                                                             |
