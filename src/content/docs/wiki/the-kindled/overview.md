title: Overview
description: An overview of the Kindled faith.
---

The faith of the Kindled splits its pantheon into Dawn Gods and Dusk Gods. The Dawn Gods wanted the best for sentient life and the Dusk Gods viewed it as a first step. The latter sought to create more powerful forms of life. To sentient peoples, these creatures are monsters.

The Pantheon has a dualist structure. Each Dawn God has a mirror in a Dusk God. They are codified as siblings and eternally war against each other for control of one of the fundamental powers.

## The Fundamental Powers

Before the world, before the gods, there were the fundamental powers. Amoral and infinite, they clashed and struck one another until there was light, then life, then thought. All things are born of them, and through them, all things are possible.

- **Arcane:** the power of creation.
- **Divine:** the power of life.
- **Martial:** the power of will.
- **Primal:** the power of nature.
- **Psionic:** the power of thought.

## The Dawn Gods

- **[Bea](./bea)** — Goddess of the Cosmos, Flame, and Passion. Aspect of the Arcane.
- **[Ira](./ira)** — God of Light, Truth, and Health. Aspect of the Divine.
- **[Petra](./petra)** — Goddess of War, Strategy, and Endurance. Aspect of the Martial.
- **[Raxus](./raxus)** — God of Beasts, Growth, and Change. Aspect of the Primal.
- **[Seren](./seren)** — Goddess of Intellect, Discipline, and Time. Aspect of the Psionic.

## The Dusk Gods

- **[Balen](./balen)** — God of the Void, Ice, and Apathy. Aspect of the Arcane.
- **[Ithild](./ithild)** — Goddess of Shadow, Lies, and Undeath. Aspect of the Divine.
- **[Peragus](./peragus)** — God of Battle, Slaughter, and Torture. Aspect of the Martial.
- **[Roxen](./roxen)** — Goddess of Monsters, Decay, and Mutation. Aspect of the Primal.
- **[Shadir](./shadir)** — God of Insanity, Chaos, and Eternity. Aspect of the Psionic.
