**Hunters of Northmarch** is a dark fantasy, monster hunting campaign. The game takes place on the continent of Orica in a medieval-inspired world.

## Rules Overview

Players will create D&D 4th edition characters for this campaign. There will be some rule changes to make several weapon and armor options more viable.

## Setting Overview

This is the minimum of what you need to know to play. Read the detailed pages on each topic for more information.

### The Empire

The Orican Empire controls the continent. In days past, southern city states warred for land and the right to rule, but now they have been unified into a republic. While Southmarch evokes qualities of the late Medieval, Northmarch is most akin to an early Medieval landscape. There's a lot of politics, expansion, and growth in the far south, but the north is poorer, less populated, and has a growing monster problem.

### Northmarch

The capital in Southmarch evokes qualities of the late Medieval, but the further north you look the more locations evoke earlier medieval aspects. In this sense, Northmarch is most akin to an early Medieval landscape.

### Gods and Monsters

There are many races of peoples across the world and many faiths, but a commonality among faiths is that the Gods created all people's in their image (humanoids). The most dominant religion is that of The Kindled Spirits. This faith splits its pantheon into Dawn Gods and Dusk Gods. The Dawn Gods wanted the best for sentient life and the Dusk Gods viewed it as a first step and sought to create more powerful forms of life. To us, these creatures are monsters.
