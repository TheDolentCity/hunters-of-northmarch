You can find the normal rules for character creation on page 14 of Player's Handbook 1. However, I am modifying these rules slightly for this campaign. Please consult the below sections for their modifications.

> **NOTE 1: No materials from Dragon Magazine are allowed unless you ask first.**

> **NOTE 2: I recommend you don't play a hybrid class.**

## Race Changes

> **NOTE: No races outside of Player's Handbooks 1-2 are allowed unless you ask first.**

Any race that has bonuses to two different ability scores (all but Human), can instead add those bonuses to _any_ ability score (but not the same one). ex: A Dragonborn could choose to add +2 to `DEX` and `WIS` to be a Ranger.

## Feat Changes

Instead of every character starting with `one` heroic tier feat, every character begins with `two` heroic tier feats. You still gain bonus feats from race or class as normal.

## Equipment Changes

You are outfitted by the Hunter's Guild. Every character begins with a steed and you have two pack mules as a group to share for excess gear. Instead of starting with gold, you can choose from the following equipment packs.

> **NOTE: Superior weapons and implements are not allowed during creation, but may be unlocked later.**

### Arcane Pack

- `1` implement
- `1` simple melee weapon
- `1` simple ranged weapon
- `light` armor
- `1` Adventurer's Pack
- `2` level 1 rituals (must have Ritual Caster feat)
- `2` pieces of adventuring gear

### Mage Pack

- `1` implement
- `1` military melee weapon **or** `1` simple melee weapon
- `1` military melee weapon **or** `1` simple ranged weapon **or** `1` shield
- `light` **or** `heavy` armor
- `1` Adventurer's Pack
- `2` pieces of adventuring gear

### Warrior Pack

- `1` military melee weapon **or** `1` simple melee weapon
- `1` military melee weapon **or** `1` simple melee weapon
- `1` military ranged weapon **or** `2` simple melee weapons
- `1` simple ranged weapon **or** `1` shield
- `light` **or** `heavy` armor
- `1` Adventurer's Pack

### Explorer Pack

- `1` military ranged weapon **or** `1` simple ranged weapon
- `1` military melee weapon **or** `2` simple melee weapons
- `2` simple melee weapons
- `light` armor
- `1` Adventurer's Pack
- `4` pieces of adventuring gear

## Mutations

Ever hunter is mutated. [Roll your random mutation here](/hunters-primer#hunter-mutations).
